Find candy crush next move

We have a candy crush board and we want to find out how many legal move we have now. a legal move means that you choose two adjacent squares and switch the candy in the two square and after the switch there is at least three adjacent squares on a line have same candy.

Input: A 2-dim list be the state of a candy crush board different number means different kinds of candy there are at most 5 kinds of candy

Output: An integer of how many legal move we have in the candy crush board

Example: 

    checkio(
    [[2,1,3,2],
     [1,1,3,3],
     [2,2,1,3],
     [1,1,3,2],
     [3,2,3,2]]) # 6
